The West Millcreek Food Pantry

Providing food and care products to those in need

Find us at 3642 W 26th St, Erie, PA 16506 behind the Westminster Presbyterian Church

The West Millcreek Food Pantry gives out staple food items on each distribution day. We provide meat, dairy, fresh produce, and other items as available. We also provide the 60+ supplemental food boxes for our neighbors age 60 and older who qualify. We mainly serve the 16505 and 16506 area codes (Millfair Rd to Pittsburgh Ave / West 38th to West 12th).

Donation Drop-off

Food donations are accepted on Mondays from 8:30 AM to 10:00 AM. All food donations should be delivered to the building behind the Westminster Presbyterian Church at the address listed above. All monetary donations should be delivered directly to the Westminster Presbyterian Church. We greatly appreciate any donations you can provide!
